🔍 What is BuildSite?

BuildSite is a web-based application that helps construction contractors and builders manage their entire project workflow — from client onboarding to final payments — in one place. It is designed specifically for the Indian construction context, with ₹ currency, multi-site project management, and support for the way builders actually work.

🔄 How the App Works — Step by Step

Here's the typical workflow a builder follows when using BuildSite:

📋 Step 1: Add Clients & Sites

Register your clients and their construction sites. Each site tracks address, status, start date.

👷 Step 2: Manage Labour

Add labourers with their trade (mason, electrician, plumber, etc.) and assign them to sites.

📐 Step 3: Plan Phases & Estimates

Break each site into construction phases. Add line-item estimates with quantities, rates, and totals.

🧱 Step 4: Track Materials

Record material purchases (item, quantity, rate, supplier). Log usage against purchases. Get automatic low-stock alerts.

💰 Step 5: Payments

Record labour payments (per labourer per site). Record client payments per phase. See payment history and totals.

📊 Step 6: Dashboard

View total investment (labour + materials), estimate vs actual per phase, labour cost by trade, material cost breakdown.

🎯 Step 7: Kanban Board

Create tasks per site. Drag & drop between To Do → In Progress → Done. Link tasks to phases and labourers.

🔔 Step 8: Reminders

Set due-date reminders for any task or milestone. Upcoming reminders and auto low-stock alerts on site page.

📱 Works on Any Device

BuildSite is a responsive web application. It works on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. No app installation needed — just open the URL in any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
