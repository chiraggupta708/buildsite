BuildSite v1 — UX Audit & Redesign

Research-backed: what Indian construction contractors actually need vs what we built. Cutting the bloat, keeping the essentials.

🔍 What Research Says Reddit, Twitter, Product Reviews, Indian Competitors (Powerplay, RDash, Onsite)
Core needs (Reddit + Indian market research): Small/mid contractors need just 4 things — track labour attendance & payments, track material costs & stock, see budget vs actual per site, know who owes what and when. Everything else is noise for v1.

😤 What Contractors Hate

  • "Too many features I'll never use" — feature bloat
  • "Why do I need a kanban board for construction?"
  • "Takes 5 clicks to see who I haven't paid"
  • "I just want labour + material tracking, not a CRM"
  • "Dashboard tells me nothing useful at a glance"
  • "My field guys can't use complex apps"
  • "Powerplay/Procore is overkill for 5-10 sites"

🛠️ What They Actually Need (v1)

  • Dashboard: all sites + financial health at one glance
  • Per site: budget vs actual spending (labour + material)
  • Labour: who worked, how many days, how much to pay
  • Materials: what was bought, what's left, stock alerts
  • Overdue payments highlighted — who hasn't been paid
  • Mobile friendly — works on phone, no training needed
  • ₹ in INR, Indian context (labour names, trades, etc)

📋 Implementation Plan — v1 Redesign
1
Simplify Sidebar
Dashboard · Clients & Sites · Labour. Remove Reminders, Kanban, Phase pages from nav. Keep them accessible via site detail tabs.
2
Redesign Dashboard
Replace the current tables with site cards. Each card = site name, client, status, progress bar (budget % spent), overdue amount. Overdue payments widget at bottom.
3
Consolidate Site Page → 3 Tabs
Overview (budget snapshot + phase table + quick actions) · Labour (cards with days/rate/paid/balance) · Materials (stock % + purchase history). Remove separate dashboard/kanban/reminders pages.
4
Add Labour Days Worked
Track attendance per labourer per site. Show cumulative days worked on the labour card. This is the #1 missing feature vs Powerplay.
5
Material Stock %
Add quantity tracking per material. Show stock remaining as % with color coding. Alert when below threshold.
Bottom line: We have 11 page.tsx files. We should cut ~4 pages (kanban, reminders, phase detail, site financial dashboard) and consolidate into 3 tabs on the site detail page. The sidebar goes from 8 nav items to 3 primary items. Dashboard goes from static tables to actionable site cards with financial health at a glance.
